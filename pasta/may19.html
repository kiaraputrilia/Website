Ella and Mama Laurie were the two people who taught me how to cook, and I will forever cherish these memories! I didn't take a lot of pictures, but they both cooked Chicken Marbella1 for dinner during one of the nights I stayed there. They also showed me a neat lil trick if I'm too tired to cook: cooking chicken fillet thighs with this sauce2. During one of those dinners, we ate arugula with sliced apples as a salad3 and it made me think about certain ingredient availabilities around the world, which I somehow didn't realize till then. During the last morning of my stay at Ella's, she taught me how to make biscuits.


It was a really nice spring day.
